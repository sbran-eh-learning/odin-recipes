Hotdogs



Candace Branham's baked chili cheese dogs!

These are a staple around the Branham house ever since Candace first tested out the recipe a couple of years back. We have experimented with a few different variations but this is the original. It's hard to improve on perfection.


Ingredients:

  • Hotdog buns
  • 100% Beef hotdogs
  • Beenless chili
  • Fritos mild cheddar cheese dip
  • OPTIONAL - Lee's coleslaw

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375Â° F
  2. Place the hotdogs only (no buns) into the pre-heated oven for about 7 minutes.
  3. Carefully remove the pan and take the hotdogs off.
  4. Lay the buns out on the heated pan and add the hotdogs, chili, cheddar cheese, and finish off with a final layer of chili.
  5. Place the pan back into the over and cook until chili is heated and the chese is good and melted (approximately 4-5 minutes).
  6. Plate the hotdogs and add on some Lee's coleslaw if you want or you could just enjoy slightly sub par hotdogs if you wish.
