Sweet Apple Treat


The Branham super secret family recipe....

My dad orginally started fixing this for our family holiday meals. It has become a family favorite and now is the go too for him to fix any time we get together as a family.


Not everyone is a fan of this though. My ex-wife was always upset that everyone always talked about the "super secret family recipe". She is now gone and we continue to enjoy this super sweet treat. No one needs that kind of negativity in their life.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 Granny Smith Apples
  • 15-18 snack size Snickers bars
  • 2 Tubs Cream Cheese Fruit dip

Steps:

  1. Core the apple and cut it into slices.
  2. Cut the apple slices into chunks. You can cut them as small or as big as you prefer
  3. Cut the snickers into chunks as well.
  4. Mix the up the apples, snickers, and fruit dip in a large bowl.
  5. Enjoy!
